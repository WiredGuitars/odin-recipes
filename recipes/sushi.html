Vegan Sushi Recipe

Description

You donâ€™t need raw fish to enjoy mouthwatering sushi! This vegan sushi recipe uses ingredients like cucumber, and avocado to create unbelievable rolls and bowls.

Ingredients

  • Rice (this is the most important ingredient, so splurge a little on your brand)
  • Carrots
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Wasabi
  • Nori

Steps

  1. Cook rice. Protip: add sugar, salt, and rice vinegar after most of the water has been absorbed by the rice
  2. Cut all veggies into matchsticks, remember avocado isn't a vegetable!
  3. Peel and mash the avocado into a separate measuring cup
  4. Add water and trace amounts of wasabi (careful with this stuff) to the avocado we just mashed.
  5. Keep mashing and mixing these three ingredients together, we're going for a creamy substance. Protip: a little peanut butter helps edge out the consistency of this creamy mixture
  6. Once the rice cools, grab a sheet of nori and compile everything onto the nori.
  7. Optimal sushi compiling goes as follows
    1. Lay sheet of nori out flat
    2. Cover 2/3 the sheet with a bed of rice
    3. Gently spread the avocado-wasabi mix over the rice
    4. Assemble the matchstick veggies in a line covering the bulk of the roll by-width
  8. And roll!
