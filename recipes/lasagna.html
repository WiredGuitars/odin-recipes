Vegan Lasagna

Description

If you’re having a bad day, there’s no way this vegan lasagna won’t turn it around for you. There’s just something about melty, gooey cheese and the aromatic nature of tomato sauce that brings smiles to faces! But, with this lasagna, you get the comfort you need with healthy ingredients mixed in (like eggplant). Don’t feel guilty about indulging a little!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Eggplant
  • Seasoning
  • Vegan meat alternative (I recommend seitan)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegan ricotta
  • Shredded vegan mozzarella
  • Marinara

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F and cook the noodles.
  2. Sauté the eggplant
  3. Cook the vegan meat
  4. Begin building the lasagna (add meat sub to bottom of cooking pan, cover bottom with lasagna noodles.
  5. Add the marinara, ricotta, top it with the mozzarrella
